About the Cookie Clicker App

The Cookie Clicker App is a fun and interactive game where players click on a cookie to earn points and upgrade their cookie production. The game features various upgrades, including hiring grandmas and Hawaiian upgrades, which help boost cookie production over time.

As players progress, they can accumulate more cookies, purchase upgrades, and unlock special golden cookies for bonus rewards. The app aims to engage users with simple mechanics while providing opportunities for strategic decision-making.
