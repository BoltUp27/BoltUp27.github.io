About the Settings Page

In Week 4, we introduced a new Settings Page to our San Diego History App. This page provides users with the ability to customize their app experience according to their preferences. Whether it’s adjusting the theme, selecting the language, or enabling notifications, this new feature aims to make the app more user-friendly and personalized.

Settings Options Available This Week

This week, we've added several new options to the Settings Page:

Theme Selection

Users can now choose between a light or dark theme for the app. This option allows for a more personalized and visually comfortable experience, whether using the app during the day or at night.

Tip: The dark theme can help reduce eye strain in low-light conditions!

Language Preferences

We’ve added a language setting that lets users switch between multiple languages, making the app accessible to a wider audience. Available languages include English, Spanish, and more coming soon!

Tip: You can change the language at any time from the settings page.

Notification Settings

The app now includes customizable notifications. Users can turn on or off notifications for important updates, new features, or events related to the San Diego History app.

Tip: Stay updated by enabling notifications for the latest app news!

Privacy & Security

We've also added privacy settings to allow users to control what data they share with the app. The new privacy settings let users opt in or out of data collection, enhancing transparency and trust.

Tip: Review your privacy settings regularly to ensure you’re comfortable with your data preferences.
