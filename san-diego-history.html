About My San Diego History App

The San Diego History app is designed to provide users with an engaging overview of the rich history of San Diego, showcasing significant events, places, and figures that shaped the city.

How It Works

Users can explore various historical topics, view interactive maps, and access detailed articles about significant events and places.

Key features include:

  • Interactive timeline of San Diego's history
  • Detailed articles and media on historical events
  • User-friendly navigation
  • Responsive design for all devices

How to Explore

To explore San Diego's history, users can:

  1. Navigate through the interactive timeline.
  2. Select a historical event to read detailed articles.
  3. View images and multimedia related to the topic.
  4. Share interesting findings with friends via social media.
